# STORES-SALES-REPORT-IN-EXCEL
# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis 2022 📊
##  Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the Vrinda Store's 2022 annual sales data. The project involves data cleaning, processing, and analyzing over 30,000+ transaction records to uncover actionable business insights. The goal of this analysis is to understand customer purchasing behaviors, demographic distributions, and the effectiveness of various sales channels to help drive strategic business decisions for the upcoming year.

# 🎯 Key Objectives
** Sales Trend Analysis: Track monthly sales revenue and order volumes to identify peak purchasing seasons.
** Demographic Profiling: Analyze purchasing patterns based on customer age groups (Adult, Senior, Teenager) and gender.
** Geographic Insights: Identify top-performing states contributing to the highest sales volumes.
** Channel Performance Evaluation: Assess the contribution of different e-commerce platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Ajio, etc.) to overall revenue.
** Order Fulfillment Rates: Evaluate the ratio of delivered orders versus cancellations and returns.

# 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
** Microsoft Excel: Utilized as the primary tool for data manipulation and analysis.
** Data Cleaning: Standardized column formats, handled missing values, and corrected data entry errors (e.g., merging spelling variations in channels).
** Advanced Formulas: Extensively used logical functions and lookup functions to categorize and segment raw data.
** Pivot Tables & Charts: Created dynamic summary tables to aggregate metrics like 'Sales vs. Orders', 'Men vs. Women', and 'Age & Gender' distributions.
** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Applied statistical and visual EDA techniques to find hidden patterns and correlations within the retail dataset.

# 📁 Dataset Structure
The analysis is broken down into several aggregated views, derived from the main Vrinda Store.csv dataset:
** Vrinda Store.csv: The core dataset containing detailed, row-level transaction data (Order IDs, SKUs, Categories, Amounts, Delivery Status, and Customer Demographics).
** Sales Vs Order.csv: Monthly breakdown comparing the total count of orders against total sales revenue.
** Men Vs Women.csv: Aggregated sales totals comparing male and female consumer spending.
** Age n Gender.csv: A matrix breaking down purchasing percentages across age groups (Teenager, Adult, Senior) and genders.
** Channels.csv: Distribution of orders across various online retail partners.
** States.csv: Total sales amounts grouped by the shipping destination state.
** Order Status.csv: Summarized counts of order outcomes (Delivered, Returned, Refunded, Cancelled).

# 💡 Key Insights Discovered
** Gender Disparity in Sales: Women accounted for a significantly larger portion of the revenue (~13.5M) compared to Men (~7.6M).
** Top Channels: Amazon and Myntra emerged as the dominant sales channels, collectively driving over 58% of the total order volume.
**Geographic Leaders: Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top three states generating the highest sales ** revenue.
Target Demographics: The "Adult" age group (particularly women) represents the most active and profitable customer segment.

# 🚀 Business Impact & Insights
This project highlights key performance indicators and consumer behaviors for Vrinda Store in 2022. By analyzing sales data across multiple dimensions (demographics, geography, and sales channels), we derived actionable insights to drive future business growth.

# 📊 Key Insights
** Overall Performance: The store successfully generated 21.17 Million in total sales revenue across 28,471 unique orders.
** Demographic Dominance: Women are the primary customer base, driving nearly 64% of total sales revenue (13.56M) compared to Men (7.61M).
** Age Group Trends: Adults lead the purchasing demographic with 10.6M in sales, followed by Teenagers (6.4M) and Seniors (4.1M).
** Top-Selling Categories: Traditional and fusion wear drive the most revenue. Sets are overwhelmingly the best-sellers (10.5M), followed by Kurtas (4.9M) and Western Dresses (3.1M).
** eographical Hotspots: The top three states contributing to revenue are Maharashtra (2.98M), Karnataka (2.64M), and Uttar Pradesh (2.10M).
** Sales Channels: Amazon is the most profitable channel, generating 7.51M in sales (~35%). Myntra (4.94M) and Flipkart (4.57M) also hold significant market share.
